1. Preparation

This is the Card Reader of Rockefeller Library‚Äôs entry gate, students have to swipe their card to go into the library.

The Card Reader is consists of a slot for card swiping, three indicator lights, wired indicate sign and covered circuits (the grey panel part).

Based on people‚Äôs interaction with the Card Reader, several questions about experience, expectation, motivation and challenge have been proposed.

2. Recording Observations

üí°Key Observations

  1. All three users swiped their cards on the Card Reader very naturally rather than tap card on the grey panel.
  1. All three users pass the gate after several (>2)swipes.
  1. People who are not familiar with the using of card reader is highly to be blocked because only swipe down and wait a sec and then swipe up they can open the gate.
  1. When people fail to swipe card, they tend to swipe with another side of card
  1. People feel nervous when they try more than 3 times

üí°Questions

  1. What do you think about this card reader?
  1. Normally, how many times do you have to swipe your card to pass the gate?
  1. (If many times) Why do you think you need to try many times?
  1. Is there any Good or Bad design on the card reader?
  1. How this design impact your experience?
  1. Compared to the inner door card reader, which one you like more?

üóíÔ∏èResponse

  • People are confused about which side of card should be used to swipe.
  • People have to try multiple times to swipe their card unless they are lucky today.
  • People believe any new user will be embarrassedly blocked in front of the gate when he/she first time come to there because of the special counterintuitive swipe method.
  • People prefer card reader with obvious mark of card swiping and think the small sign on the Rockefeller‚Äôs card reader is helpless, and the whole design is a disaster.

3. Personas

Liam:

  • Liam is a senior student in Brown University and he often go to the Rockefeller library for study. Today, Liam rushes into the Rockefeller Library for study because he get the final exam tomorrow.
  • He is not sure about which side of card should be used to swipe
  • This persona represents majority of students who have been to the Rockefeller Library before, they know how to swipe card in that special card reader but still cannot memory which side should be used.

  • He don‚Äôt know how to swipe the card correctly no matter swipe the correct side.
